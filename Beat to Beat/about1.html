Welcome

Beat To Beat Dance Crew

It is a institute of art and motion which was established by Dance and Art Director Omnkar Bhaskar Rokade on 1st April 2013. The main purpose behind its establishment is to make Art easily available at local and global level. It involves various courses of Dance and Theater forms along with advance techniques and innovative ideas. Training is provided for Ladies, Kids, Special Children, Gents, Couples, & Groups without any age restrictions.

Till the date, more than 1500 members are connected with us from Sangli and other cities.

4 Years before in 2013, We have started this dance institute with intention of expand love for dance.

Omnkar Rokade, a young dynamic art and dance director who having more than 15 years of experience in dance and art area. Omnkar & Team has won many prizes & having experties in dance type like Zumba, Classical, Folk, hiphop, Contemporary, Swag.
